2025-1791 NOW THEREFORE IT BE RESOLVED, the Council of the City of Pittsburgh

funding to Pittsburgh Regional Transit by at least $117 million in the 2026 fiscal year
to prevent a catastrophic loss of transit service; and,

distributed to the Allegheny County delegations of the Pennsylvania State General
Assembly and to the office of Pennsylvania Governor Josh Shapiro.
